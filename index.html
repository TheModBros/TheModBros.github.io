S Plus Group | Entertainment | Gaming

A New Start

S Plus Gaming started up as TheModBros. A group of talented students dedicated to gaming, committed to enhancing the gaming experience.

Originally formed in 2011, two young minds wanted to share their skills in Minecraft. In 2012, those two collaboratively gained more friends to join in their journey. Later in 2012, the newly forming team began working on Minecraft maps and skins.

Skip two years, the team move on from maps and modpacks to making mods. Mods that change the gameplay all together, adding items, biomes, blocks, entities, etc. etc.

Soon after, original group known as TheModBros banded with S Plus Group to create the S Plus Gaming section, within the larger S Plus Entertainment. While S Plus Safety and Schooling focuses on the older generation of parents, S Plus Entertainment Gaming focuses on the younger generation of kids and teenagers.

About a year later, the head coder started doing websites, expanding his knowledge from simple Java to even simpler HTML and CSS and some jQuery. Soon to start working on Swift, the CTO is excited of what's next to come.

Despite our original primary game being Minecraft, the team has broadened out, focusing on gameplay footage in games like Grand Theft Auto V, various Pokémon games, Super Smash Bros 4, and many many others.

We look forward to the future of this company. We have hope that working with the S Plus Group can give us more opportunities than when we were on our own.
