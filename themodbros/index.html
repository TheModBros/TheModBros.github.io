TheModBros are a group of talented gaming students committed to enhancing and entertaining people of the world. We base our works in Hong Kong SAR, China. We speak English and some Chinese.

Originally formed in 2011 as a way for 2 young minds to share our Minecraft skills. TheModBros started off only providing Minecraft mod packs. In early 2012, EDR and JackieBoi asked more friends to join the team. At that time, not many wanted to join. Later that year, We started working on Minecraft maps and skins.

Skip to 2014, we have moved from working on only maps and modpacks, to making full on mods, ready to play whenever you want. It's been a while, but our dreams of this company have finally been completed. We know how to mod and are releasing them on this website.

Skip over to the summer of 2015, we learned more languages. From simple HTML and CSS to a little more complicated jQuery, to a little more JavaScript. However the only Java we know is primarily just Minecraft Mod Coding. This website is even one of the other products created by the head coder, Edward. We're even thinking of learning Swift, to begin creating iOS Apps.


We are sincerely sorry for our lack of content. We're still students in school and we still need to focus on our academics. We're working as hard as we can on the side. But frankly we don't have a lot of time on our hands. But this is what we love to do, and we're not going to stop until we're done with all our projects.
