Mods/Vanilla Plus

Vanilla Plus is a mod created by TheModBros, as a additional items based mod. With 68 additional tools and weapons, and 7 different armors based around 7 different Vanilla materials in game. The 7 materials that have so much potential to be used for items and armors, but aren't. For example, Obsidian tools/armors, Emerald, Lapis, etc.
